*** Application Purpose ***

Application uses geolocation, csv parsing, and logging
to determine the distance of two given Taco Bells.


*** Application Flow ***
Parse the CSV:

Calculate Distances:
Read the file to get a list of Taco Bell locations, each having latitude and longitude.

Determine Maximum Distance:
For each pair of Taco Bell locations, compute the distance.

Logging:
Keep track of the maximum distance and the corresponding pair of locations.
Log the result, including the two locations and the distance between them.
